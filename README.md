Because apparently Google Drive, GitHub Gists, Notion, and the 47 random .txt files on your desktop weren't enough places to lose your code snippets.

## What Fresh Hell Is This?

A browser-based snippet manager that stores everything in LocalStorage. You know, that thing you clear every time your browser acts weird? Yeah, that one.

Perfect for developers who:
- Copy-paste the same 5 functions across 20 projects
- Save snippets they'll "definitely use later" (narrator: they won't)
- Trust their browser more than they trust cloud storage
- Enjoy living dangerously

## Features (And Why They're Barely Useful)

**LocalStorage Persistence** üì¶  
All snippets stored in your browser! Until you:

- Switch browsers

Then it's all gone. Hope you weren't attached to that regex you finally got working.

**Real-time Search** üîç  
Find that snippet you saved 6 months ago and never used! Search by title, language, or that one variable name you definitely remember... was it `userData` or `user_data`? Who knows!

**Auto-save** üíæ  
Never manually save again! The app does it for you. Until your browser crashes mid-type and you lose everything anyway. But hey, at least we tried.

**Language Selection** üåê

**Clipboard Integration** üìã

**Responsive Design** üì±
